SAUDI ARABIA REGULATORY COMPLIANCE

Required Standards:
- SASO 2664: Street lighting luminaires requirements
- SASO 2892: Electrical safety standards for outdoor lighting
- Tarsheed: Energy efficiency and conservation program
- Municipal lighting regulations

Certification Process:
- SASO Certificate of Conformity mandatory
- Energy Efficiency Label required
- Quality Management Certificate (ISO 9001)
- Third-party testing reports required
